How It Works

Your second chance at a real connection

Backtrack makes it simple to find people you've noticed at real locations. Here's how.

1

Notice Someone

You're at your regular coffee shop, the gym, a bookstore, or any public place. You notice someone interesting - maybe you make eye contact, share a smile, or just appreciate them from afar.

This is the moment Backtrack is built for. That spark of interest that usually leads nowhere now has a chance to become something more.

2

Create Your Post

Open Backtrack and search for the location where you saw them. Our Google Maps integration helps you find the exact venue.

Then, use our avatar builder to describe what they looked like. Pick from 11+ features: hair color, style, skin tone, glasses, clothing, and more. No photos needed - just select the features that match.

Write a short note about the moment. Something like "You were reading something that made you laugh. Would love to know what it was."

Optionally, take a quick selfie to verify you were actually there. This photo is never shown to anyone - it just proves authenticity.

3

They Find Your Post

Here's where the magic happens. When the person you noticed opens Backtrack and browses posts at that location, they might see your post.

Our smart matching algorithm helps. If they've created their own avatar (describing themselves), we highlight posts that closely match their appearance. Higher match scores mean "this post might be about you."

They read your note, look at your avatar description, and think... "Wait, that sounds like me. I was there on Tuesday. Could this be...?"

4

Connect & Chat

If they recognize themselves in your description, they can respond to your post. Congratulations - you've matched!

Now you can chat anonymously. Messages are delivered instantly with read receipts. You can share photos in the conversation when you're comfortable.

Your real identity stays private until you decide to share more. Maybe you exchange names after a few messages. Maybe you agree to meet back at that coffee shop. The next step is up to both of you.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if they're not on the app?

Your post will be there waiting. As more people join Backtrack, the chances of finding your missed connection increase. In the meantime, you've done your part.

Is my identity revealed?

Never without your consent. You chat using avatars, and only share your real identity when you're both comfortable.

Can someone find out where I've been?

No. We don't track your location. Others can only see posts you've created, not where you've browsed.

What if someone makes a fake post?

Selfie verification (optional but encouraged) proves you were at the location. Plus, our matching algorithm and moderation help filter out spam.

Is this just for dating?

Backtrack is for any missed connection - romantic, friendly, professional. The app facilitates connection; you decide what kind.

How accurate is avatar matching?

Our algorithm weighs 11+ attributes with primary features (skin, hair) carrying more weight. Match scores indicate likelihood, not certainty.
