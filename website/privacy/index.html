Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 30, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Backtrack. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, password (encrypted), date of birth
  • Avatar Data: Custom avatar configurations you create
  • User Content: Posts, messages, and photos you upload

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Location Data: GPS location when you create posts (only with permission)
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, unique identifiers
  • Usage Data: Features you use, interactions within the app

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Enable location-based post discovery
  • Match you with other users based on avatar descriptions
  • Facilitate messaging between matched users
  • Send push notifications about matches and messages

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information.

  • With Other Users: Your avatar and posts are visible at locations
  • Service Providers: Supabase (database), Google Maps (location), Expo (notifications)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law

5. Your Rights

  • Access: View your data in app settings
  • Correction: Update your profile anytime
  • Deletion: Delete your account in Profile > Delete Account
  • Location: Disable location access in device settings

6. Data Security

We implement encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest, secure password hashing, and Row-Level Security policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Backtrack is for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

8. Contact Us

For privacy questions: privacy@backtrack.social

Data Protection Officer (EEA): dpo@backtrack.social
