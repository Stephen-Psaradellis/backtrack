Features

Privacy-first by design

Every feature in Backtrack was built to protect your privacy while helping you make real connections.

Avatar-Based Matching

Our unique avatar builder lets you describe someone using 11+ customizable features - hair color, style, skin tone, glasses, clothing, and more. No photos required means complete privacy for everyone.

  • 11+ customizable avatar attributes
  • Primary features: skin, hair, facial hair (weighted 60%)
  • Secondary features: eyes, clothes, accessories (weighted 40%)
  • Smart matching algorithm finds relevant posts
  • No real photos needed for matching

Anonymous Until Ready

When you match with someone, start chatting without revealing your identity. Share what you want, when you want. You're always in control.

  • No real names shared until you choose
  • Avatar-based chat interface
  • Optional photo sharing in conversations
  • Block or unmatch anytime
  • Your identity, your rules

Real Locations

Posts are tied to actual venues you visit - coffee shops, gyms, bookstores, bars, parks, and more. Google Maps integration helps you find the exact place.

  • Google Maps integration for venue search
  • Browse posts at specific locations
  • No location tracking - you choose where to post
  • Discover posts near you or anywhere
  • Venue-specific missed connections

Real-Time Chat

When you match, start chatting instantly. Messages are delivered in real-time with read receipts, typing indicators, and in-app photo sharing.

  • Instant message delivery
  • Read receipts and typing indicators
  • In-app photo sharing
  • Push notifications for new messages
  • Conversation history preserved

Safety & Moderation

Your safety is our priority. We require age verification, moderate all shared images, and make it easy to block or report anyone.

  • 18+ age verification required
  • AI-powered image moderation
  • One-tap blocking and reporting
  • Community guidelines enforced
  • Quick response to reports

Your Data, Your Control

We believe your data belongs to you. Delete your account and all associated data anytime with one tap. GDPR compliant.

  • One-tap account deletion
  • All data deleted within 30 days
  • GDPR compliant
  • No data selling, ever
  • Transparent privacy policy
